FAQ

Who is BDSound for?

Whether you want a volume booster , bass booster, better sound quality, or an easy-to-use Windows EQ, we've got you covered.

Our users are budget audiophiles, music lovers, gamers, transcriptionists, Netflix bingers, and more. Especially if you're using quiet laptop speakers or other low quality audio hardware, FxSound is the best free software to boost volume, bass, and sound quality. And no free EQ for Windows is easier to use than FxSound.

Why is BDSound free and how do you guys make money?

For over 20 years we've been selling BDSound (we used to call it DFX Audio Enhancer) for unrestricted access to our sound boosting software.

But we've recently decided to make BDSound 100% free and open-source. Why? Because our mission is to make high quality audio accessible to all, not just those who can afford our software or expensive speakers and sound systems.

And we can do this only with your help. BDSound is now entirely supported by our users. Click here to donate to support our continued development efforts.

How does FxSound work?

Home stereo systems and PC’s are built with a compromise: keep hardware affordable at the cost of quality.

BDSound compensates for low quality hardware by targeting and processing sound’s timbre, volume, spatial balance, and dynamics

What this means for you is clearer, louder, deeper, and balanced audio. Install our lightweight program, let it run quietly in the background, and enjoy your new audio experience.
